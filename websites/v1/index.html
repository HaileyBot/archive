ABOUT

Key features of The Network

Function

The Network is designed to bring people together, specifically people who would never have met or ever talked to each other. Links are formed between various chat servers, enabling communication across multiple platforms.

Connection

The Network forms connections between people, breaking barriers and allowing relationships to form and grow.

Design

Our design is something that took time and effort. We take pride in our design, and are open to suggestions regarding improvements that can be made.

Support

We offer instant support approximately 15 hours per day, and the wait time when instant support isnt available shouldn't exceed 4 hours. We have a quick response time and offer solutions that actually help.
